Frequently asked questions

What makes Walcart different from other retailers?

Walcart focuses on a curated selection of everyday essentials with transparent pricing and no membership fees — you get retailer-level prices without the retailer-level markup.

Does Walcart offer free shipping?

Yes — free shipping is included on all orders over $35, with no minimum order requirement for local pickup.

How fast is delivery?

Most orders arrive within 2–3 business days, with same-day pickup available at select locations.

Is there a membership fee?

No. Walcart has zero membership fees — every customer gets the same low prices, no subscription required.

What is Walcart's return policy?

Unopened items can be returned within 30 days for a full refund, no questions asked.

Does Walcart sell locally sourced products?

Yes — wherever possible, Walcart partners with regional suppliers to offer fresher goods and support local economies.
